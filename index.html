  • RocBoi Quez

New Single: "Stairs or Window"

RocBoi Quez returns with his electrifying new single "Stairs or Window", produced by Terence Mix of Sur.real Productions. This single is a bold ballot paying homage to the classic urban film "I'm Gonna Git You Sucka". RocBoi delivers a potent message — offering competitors a choice: fall hard or rise with the Slattland movement only.

Straight from the South, RocBoi Quez lyrically slices through the underground scene with a style that's uniquely southern, yet globally magnetic. Critics compare his delivery to the legacy of Ludacris (DTP), LL COOL J, and a swagger reminiscent of RocNation.

As Slattland Records reemerges with a renewed vision, "Stairs or Window" marks its first official release — a statement to the industry that business meets artistry. RocBoi declares: Let’s flood the market.

With Bam of Sur.real Production on the beat, blending electronic energy with raw hip-hop rhythm, this Atlanta x Alabama collaboration defines a new sonic wave that is rooted in authenticity and style.

RocBoi Quez

RocBoi Quez is a dynamic force in the hip-hop scene, emerging from Atlanta with a gritty style and a story that resonates. Known for his raw lyrics and undeniable presence, Quez is set to release his powerful new single “Stairs or Window.” Self-distributed through Tunecore and self-published under G Code Publishing, the single reflects the struggle between elevation and escape, a metaphorical look into choices and survival. RocBoi Quez is more than an artist—he’s a movement.

About Slattland Records

Established in 2020, Slattland Records is an independent label dedicated to discovering, promoting, and elevating the next generation of music pioneers. Located in Atlanta, Ga., Slattland Records is currently seeking engineers, music-producers, and Artists/songwriters that are serious about their craft. We are currently accepting Demos and Bios to all that are interested.
